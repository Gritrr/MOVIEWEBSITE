Spy x Family: Code White

The plot revolves around the family coming together to thwart a new and dangerous threat to world peace. The central character, Loid Forger (codename: Twilight), is a spy working under the guise of a family man to gather intelligence and prevent global conflict. His wife, Yor, is a highly skilled assassin, and their adopted daughter, Anya, is a telepath who can read minds, though the family members are unaware of each other's true identities. The storyline centers on an incident involving a criminal group, and Loid must go on a mission to protect national security while also maintaining his facade as a loving father and husband. The film includes action-packed spy sequences, humor, and emotional moments as the family struggles to keep their secrets while working together to resolve the crisis.
