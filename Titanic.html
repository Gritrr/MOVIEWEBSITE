Titanic

Directed by James Cameron, is a romantic drama set against the backdrop of the real-life sinking of the RMS Titanic in 1912. The film tells the story of two passengers from different social classes who fall in love aboard the ill-fated ship. The movie primarily follows Jack Dawson, a poor artist from Wisconsin, and Rose DeWitt Bukater, an aristocratic young woman engaged to the wealthy but controlling Caledon 'Cal' Hockley. They meet on the Titanic, and despite the societal pressures and her engagement, they develop a passionate love affair. As the Titanic sails from Southampton to New York, disaster strikes when the ship hits an iceberg. The latter part of the film focuses on the ship's tragic sinking, the chaos and panic that ensue, and the emotional struggles of Jack and Rose as they fight for survival.

Genre: Romantic, Fantasy

Release Year: 1997
